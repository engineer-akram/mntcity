About M&T Section

The M&T (Metering & Testing) section provides specialized tools for meter testing, physical checking, and monitoring activities within GEPCO City Circle Gujranwala.

  • Physical Checking: Tools for on-site meter inspections and verifications
  • Activity Monitoring: Track and manage M&T operations and progress
  • Report Generation: Create detailed reports for analysis and compliance
  • Performance Tracking: Monitor individual and team performance metrics

For assistance or technical issues, please contact the M&T department.
