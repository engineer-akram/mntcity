About Operation Section

The Operation section provides tools for Sub Divisions, Divisions, and Revenue Offices to manage data, view reports, and monitor M&T activities within GEPCO City Circle Gujranwala.

  • Data Management: Update and maintain operational data
  • Report Monitoring: Track progress of M&T activities and reports
  • MCO Statements: Manage Meter Change Orders and statements
  • Abstract Reports: Generate detailed abstracts for various checking categories
  • Meter Tracking: Monitor meters received in labs and CP90 processes

This section is specifically designed for operational staff including Sub Division officers, Division heads, and Revenue Office personnel.
